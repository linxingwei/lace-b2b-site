# About page image needs

The first published version uses verified product-display photographs already supplied by Velorace Lace. These images are described as product selection, embellishment and workmanship examples; they are not presented as factory evidence.

Replace or add the following when original company photographs are available:

- `factory-production.webp` — a real wide photograph of the embroidery or lace production area.
- `embroidery-machine.webp` — a real computer embroidery machine in operation.
- `hand-beading.webp` — an authorized photograph of beadwork or hand-finishing.
- `bridal-application.webp` — an authorized wedding gown or couture application photograph.
- `quality-inspection.webp` — a real inspection, measurement or final-check scene.
- `export-packing.webp` — a real packing or shipment-preparation scene.

Recommended source size: at least 1600 px on the long edge, exported as WebP or AVIF. Do not use supplier, customer or factory photographs without permission.
